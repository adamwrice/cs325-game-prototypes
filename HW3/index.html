  • High Concept: I wanted a unique platforming experience where the players could work together to get through obstacles.
  • Theme: Anniversary of the Last Day of School, Letters Returned, and Winged Person
  • Mandated Variety: Randomized Cards
  • Prototype Goal: Implement gamestates and a story
  • Player Experience Goals: Memorization and matching
  • Gameplay: Choose how many cards you want to match and then you have 3 minutes to match them all or lose
  • Strategies: Click one and look for its match, but make sure to remember ones you've already been shown
  • Story/Setting/Premise: It's the 1st North Point High School Reunion and everyone has letters they've written to themselves on their last day of school. A year later, the letters are being distributed again! You are in charge of making sure everyone gets their letter!
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
  • Play Time: 3-5 minutes
