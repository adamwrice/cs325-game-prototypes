  • High Concept: I wanted a unique platforming experience where the players could work together to get through obstacles.
  • Theme: Mutliplayer platformer
  • Mandated Variety: None
  • Prototype Goal: To use tiles to have a moving background, implement music, and collideable sprites that could jump off each other.
  • Player Experience Goals: Teamwork and platforming skills.
  • Gameplay: Players work together to find the triangle.
  • Strategies: Hop off each other to reach platforms that are higher up.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: An astronaut and a magic living square are trying to find the square's lost lover, a triangle.
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
